I've met a lot of people lately who keep telling me they want to do great things in life and ask me to guide them or give them advice,  which I do with full honesty.
I give them a lot of options and even a path they can follow, at least for now. But rather than starting out, they keep thinking.
And they believe that "thinking" is rewarding but it's nothing more than procrastination imo. This is a trap. They're just making excuses to stay in their comfort zone. Because if they make a decision, they need to start working, and the brain doesn't want that. It just wants COMFORT.
And they keep lying to no one but themselves. Like wtf bro? At least be honest with yourself.
